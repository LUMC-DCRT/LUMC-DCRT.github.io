Is the variant of a type applicable to be evaluated with these guidelines?

Variants applicable to these guidelines are restricted to single nucleotide variants, small indels, one or multi-exon deletions or duplications, and full gene deletion or duplication. Currently excluded are variants in non-coding genes, deletions/duplications spanning multiple genes (i.e., contiguous gene syndromes), imprinting defects/uniparental disomy, structural rearrangements (e.g., translocations), and aneuploidies. Please also see “Section 0” of the N1C consensus guidelines for further explanations.
